The Suspension Bridge, Vancouver, Canada

Temperature: Loading...

Condition: Loading...

Trip Date: July, 2023

Vancouver is a city that perfectly blends urban living with natural beauty. One of the highlights was walking across the Capilano Suspension Bridge, a great experience that offered stunning views of the green forest and the rushing river far below. The gentle sway of the bridge made the adventure thrilling. The surrounding park added to the charm with its towering trees and the peaceful ambiance of nature. Vancouver is a city where you can go from the beach to the mountain in the same day, making it truly unforgettable.

Banff, Alberta, Canada

Trip Date: May, 2024

Banff was a fun family trip filled with hiking, stunning views, and unforgettable moments. It’s located in the heart of the Canadian Rockies, a breathtaking destination filled with rugged mountains, crystal-clear lakes, and diverse wildlife. I love the hiking trails, and the relaxing nature. The natural beauty of the Canadian Rockies and the clear blue lakes make it a must-see. Bring a good camera and comfortable shoes as there’s so much to explore!
